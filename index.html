Biography

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Evangeline F. Y. Young. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree in the Department of Electronic and Communication Engineering from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) in 2015.

My research interests include combinatorial optimization, numerical optimization, and design automation.
